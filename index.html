Synopsis

What happens when the NBA and Looney Tunes collide? You get the biggest movie of 1996, Space Jam! Michael Jordan stars in this blockbuster alongside cartoon icon Bugs Bunny. After being brought into the world of Looney Tunes, Jordan is enlisted to help save it from the Monstars. Can Jordan and the Tunes rise to the occasion and save their beloved world? This is the one flick you can't afford to miss!
